This Power BI dashboard analyzes BilTeq Hotel's booking performance from 2012 to 2021, offering insights into key metrics like gross and net booking fees, customer demographics, and profit trends. It features interactive slicers for year, gender, origin country, and payment mode, alongside visualizations such as the top 5 most visited and origin countries, year-on-year profit analysis, and customer segmentation by gender and age. The dashboard provides a comprehensive tool for stakeholders to make data-driven decisions, with a focus on accessibility and actionable insights.
